    ABOUT US

    Sign2Speech uses computer vision and deep learning to detect sign language, providing real-time audio and visual outputs for inclusive communication for those with hearing impairments.

    What to expect.

    Real-time Sign Language Interpretation: Leveraging advanced computer vision techniques, our project swiftly recognizes and interprets sign language gestures in real-time.

    Seamless Conversion to Speech: Through sophisticated algorithms, these detected gestures are promptly translated into spoken language, facilitating immediate communication for individuals with hearing impairments.

    Empowering Inclusivity: By bridging the gap between sign language and speech, our project aims to create an inclusive environment where communication barriers for people with hearing impairments are dismantled, promoting equality and understanding.
